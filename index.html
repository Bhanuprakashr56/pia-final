The Task

Pick one of the two companies. Deliver a product teardown — your 5 sharpest feedbacks across the Product Pillars below. This is not a filter exercise; whoever joins owns and executes these from Day 1.

Use SWOT / 7Ps / Porter's Five Forces or any framework at your disposal.

Structure each feedback:

(a) Observed
What you actually saw in the app / website / flow
(b) Problem
Why it matters — the real user or business pain
(c) Ship instead
Your specific, actionable solution

How We'll Evaluate

Moves Forward
  • Real, non-obvious observation
  • Specific screens / copy / flows
  • Tradeoff thinking
  • Clear observation → problem → solution chain
  • Prioritisation logic
Doesn't Move Forward
  • Generic feedback ("improve UX", "add gamification")
  • All 5 in one area
  • Features without an observed problem
  • Obvious signs the app wasn't used
  • Polished but shallow

We don't care about CV brand. We care whether you used the app, thought hard, and can communicate it clearly.
